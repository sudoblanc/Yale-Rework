About Us

Through a combination of coursework, studio practice, and critical engagement, students develop their own artistic voice and vision. Our faculty of practicing artists and scholars are committed to fostering a culture of experimentation, innovation, and dialogue that pushes students to explore new boundaries and take risks.

At the Yale School of Art, we believe that art has the power to transform individuals, communities, and the world. We are proud to be a part of this vibrant and dynamic community, and we invite you to join us in our mission to create meaningful and impactful art.
